Vision

A God-Centered City, Learning Center, Vibrant Business Hub, Premier Eco-tourism Destination in Northwestern Mindanao with empowered stakeholders living in a peaceful, progressive and happy environment spearheaded by committed and competent servant leaders.

Mission

To integrate and ensure the delivery of quality basic services and promote eco-tourism investment through good governance in order to uplift the living standard of the people.
